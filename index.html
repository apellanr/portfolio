About Me

My name is Ryan and I'm a Web Developer based out of Orange County, CA. After graduating from UCI, I pursued a career to become a dental professional, but realized that such an occuption was not my passion. My eagerness and curiousity, which are key attributes that propel my ambitions, directed me towards another pathway.
I discovered myself veering towards my creative qualities and past inspirations. To put it into perspective, 'Xanga' gave me the opportunity to build my first webpage in middle school (talk about throwback). I was always excited to learn about the new and popular programs being developed as technology grew.
I pivoted careers paths for personal growth and for a chance to be myself. I have great interpersonal skills and work sufficiently in a team and/or individual environment. Let's connect and start building!
